Rules

To start, you'll be given four letters. Unscramble them into a word by tapping the letter tiles in order. Once you do, a fifth letter will be revealed. Repeat the process, unscrambling a five-letter word, then a six-letter word, and so on, all the way up to nine.

Each word must use every available letter exactly once.

There may be multiple acceptable words at any given level, but you only need to enter one to advance.

The game ends once you unscramble a nine letter word.

Scoring
You'll solve five puzzles in a row. For each puzzle, you'll have a maximum of four minutes to solve.
Solve as fast as you can -- your times will be saved to a leaderboard.

When you complete a puzzle, your timer will pause until the next puzzle begins.

If you do not complete the puzzle in 4 minutes, an additional 45 seconds will be added to your score per level you missed.

If you're stuck, you can double-tap the "Show Next Letter" button to reveal the next letter of a word. You can use this feature as much as you like, but it'll add 30 seconds to your score each time.
You can also Clear or Delete tapped-in letters using the buttons provided, or shuffle your letters using the 🔄 button. There are no penalties for using these buttons.
Important
Once you start the game, do not leave, minimize, or refresh the site or else your progress may be lost.
Do not tap the "Begin" button below until instructed to do so.

Good luck!

P.S. No, the emojis are not part of the game. They're just there to look cute.
